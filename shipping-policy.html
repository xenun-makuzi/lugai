Shipping Policy

We are committed to providing secure, fast, and reliable shipping for every order placed through LugaiTCG. Every collectible is packaged with care to ensure it arrives safely and in the condition advertised.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays. Most orders are processed within one to three business days after payment has been successfully verified.

  • Orders placed during weekends or holidays begin processing on the next business day.
  • High-demand product launches may require additional processing time.
  • Orders containing multiple items may ship together.
  • We reserve the right to delay shipment if fraud prevention verification is required.

2. Shipping Methods

LugaiTCG partners with trusted shipping carriers to deliver your purchases safely.

  • USPS
  • UPS
  • FedEx
  • DHL (International)

Carrier selection depends on your shipping destination, package size, and available services.

3. Estimated Delivery Times

Destination Estimated Time
United States 2–7 Business Days
Canada 5–10 Business Days
Europe 7–14 Business Days
Australia 8–16 Business Days
Rest of World 10–21 Business Days

4. Tracking Information

Once your shipment has been dispatched, a confirmation email containing your tracking number will be sent to the email address used during checkout.

Tracking information may require up to 24 hours before becoming active on the carrier's website.

5. Packaging Standards

Every collectible is carefully packaged using professional shipping materials including:

  • Protective sleeves
  • Top loaders
  • Bubble wrap
  • Cardboard reinforcement
  • Water-resistant packaging
  • Heavy-duty shipping boxes

Graded cards receive additional protection to minimize movement during transit.

6. International Shipping

International customers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, import fees, and local regulations imposed by their destination country.

Delivery times may vary depending on customs inspections.

7. Incorrect Shipping Address

Customers are responsible for providing accurate shipping information before placing an order.

Orders returned due to incorrect addresses may require additional shipping fees before reshipment.

8. Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged or appears to be lost in transit, please contact our customer support within seven (7) days of the expected delivery date.

Please include your order number, photographs of any damage, and any supporting information so our support team can begin an investigation immediately.

9. Shipping Restrictions

  • We reserve the right to refuse shipment where prohibited by law.
  • Some products may not be available for shipment to certain countries.
  • Additional identity verification may be required for high-value purchases.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Shipping Policy, please contact our customer support team.
