1. Information We Collect

When you use LugaiTCG, we may collect information necessary to process your orders, improve our services, and provide customer support.

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Order history
  • Payment confirmation details
  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Website usage statistics
We collect only the information necessary to provide our services and improve your shopping experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us operate LugaiTCG efficiently and securely.

  • Process orders.
  • Verify payments.
  • Ship purchases.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Respond to inquiries.
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized transactions.
  • Improve website performance.
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and enhance your browsing experience.

  • Remember shopping cart contents.
  • Store user preferences.
  • Analyze website traffic.
  • Improve website performance.
  • Protect against fraudulent activity.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, certain features of the website may not function properly.

4. Payment Security

Protecting your financial information is extremely important to us.

LugaiTCG does not permanently store complete payment card information on our servers. Payment processing is handled using secure, encrypted payment technologies designed to protect customer information.

Never send sensitive payment information by email unless specifically instructed through a secure communication channel.

5. Sharing Your Information

LugaiTCG respects your privacy and does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share limited information only when necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Shipping carriers to deliver your orders.
  • Secure payment processors to complete transactions.
  • Fraud prevention and security providers.
  • Website hosting and technical service providers.
  • Government authorities when legally required.
Any third-party service provider receiving your information is required to protect it and use it only for the services they provide on our behalf.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete your orders.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Maintain accurate business records.
  • Meet tax and legal obligations.
  • Resolve disputes when necessary.

When personal information is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information.

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of eligible personal data.
  • Request a copy of the information we maintain.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our customer support team using the contact information provided below.

8. Third-Party Services & Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services for your convenience.

These websites operate independently and maintain their own privacy practices. LugaiTCG is not responsible for the content, security, or privacy policies of external websites.

We encourage customers to review the privacy policies of any third-party website before providing personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

LugaiTCG is intended for users who are at least 13 years of age or the minimum age required by applicable law.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental or guardian consent.

If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child in violation of applicable law, we will promptly delete that information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our business practices, legal requirements, or website functionality.

When significant changes are made, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly.

Continued use of our website after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
